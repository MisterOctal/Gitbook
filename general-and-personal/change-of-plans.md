# Change of Plans

Written on: 07.03.2026

Unfortunately, a few days ago, it was announced that the UK government has decided to suspend student visa applications for all Myanmar citizens. This means that my future has been turned around, I wanted to apply for UCB (University College Birmingham) because of their extensive industry-alignments and hands-on lab work but that dream is off the table.

Since I was a kid, I've always wanted to go to the UK and experience their education system. My whole life I've studied British-aligned curriculums such as IGCSE and now HND. Despite this unfortunate setback, my resolve is stronger than ever.

I've decided Canada, specifically BCIT (British Columbia Institute of Technology) might be the best aligned for my future. It's another industry-aligned, highly vocational institute just like UCB that's extremely HND-friendly.

This setback is devastating and sad but I cannot give up. I will try my best, expanding my portfolio, studying for Security+, and acing my HND to make sure I can pursue education abroad.
